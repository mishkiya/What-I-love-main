The Forty Springs

Founded in 1955, this stunning natural site holds great historical significance. Arbaminch features over 40 pristine springs that provide pure, drinkable water. The natural springs flow with pure water in a memorizing display of nature's Purity.

Crocodile Ranch

It is home to over 2,500 crocodiles, offering visitors a chance to observe these reptiles in their natural habitat. tour guides share fascinating facts about crocodile biology, making it an educational experience.

Nechsar Park

A haven for wildlife surrounded by Lakes Abaya and Chamo, The Park is a paradise for wildlife with diverse ecosystems. It is reknowned for its different types of fishes in the lakes and vibrant birdlife, best appreciated from above.
